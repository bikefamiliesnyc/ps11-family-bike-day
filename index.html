About This Event

PS11 Family Bike Day is organized by Bike Families NYC and the PS11 PTA to celebrate biking as a way to build healthier, happier, more connected communities. PS11 (The Purvis J. Behan School) is a beloved public elementary school in the heart of Clinton Hill, Brooklyn.

Bike Families NYC brings together families across Brooklyn to ride, advocate, and build community through cycling.
